Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time

45 minutes

Serving Size

8 people

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
2. Grease and flour two cake pans
3. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl
4. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla
5. Stir in boiling water
6. Pour batter into pans and bake 30-35 minutes
